2021 Beta Challenge

XFC started in 2021 with the goal of exposing students to the power of Genetic Fuzzy Systems, while also highlighting the explainability inherent to these types of systems. The first challenge involved creating an AI agent to play the game Asteroid Smashers. Your algorithm would control a single ship through increasingly difficult scenarios to evaulate it's effectiveness.
